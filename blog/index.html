Melatonin released!

published 2014.07.16

Actually, I decided to publish Melatonin to the chrome store in the beginning of june. Since then, I've published 4 new updates; most notably v0.2.4 - the most recent - which improves the algorithm for the altitude calculations used by Melatonin.

I created Melatonin for my own use, having found the alternatives for Chrome OS unsatisfying in terms of usability (responsive interface) and features (automatic color). F.lux has sane defaults and good automation, I wanted these things for Melatonin coupled with a minimalistic interface.

Currently, that interface is perhaps too minimalistic; sacrificing power for simplicity. In update v0.3.0 I hope to have constructed a better comprimise between the two.
